Minister In-Charge


Pr. Moncy P. Mathew was born & brought up in Chittar, Pathanamthitta. After his secular education he joined Grace Bible College, Harayana in the year 1988. He graduated in 1991 and for the past 28 years he has been serving the Lord as a Pastor as part of Church Ministry.

In 1994 he got married to Sr. Lovely Varghese (Shirley). Loise Moncy was his first daughter who went to be with the Lord in the year 2014. Reba Moncy his second daughter and Aaron Moncy are currently pursuing their education.

Pastor Moncy P. Mathew was associated with various churches at the following places:

  1. Mota Nagar, New Delhi
  2. Velakulangra, Adoor
  3. Nellimukal, Adoor
  4. Adoor Town
  5. Thrikkanamangal, Kottarakara
  6. Nellikkamon, Ranni
  7. Abu Dhabi
  8. Kozhencherry
Since April 2019, Pastor Moncy P. Mathew is the residential pastor of IPC Full Gospel Church Kuwait.
